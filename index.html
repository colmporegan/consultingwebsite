My story

What is Lighthouse AI Consulting?

The name Lighthouse Consulting takes its inspiration from the lighthouse that stands on Fastnet Rock off the southwest coast of Ireland. For over a century, it has guided ships safely through some of the most challenging waters in the North Atlantic.

That's the idea behind Lighthouse AI Consulting. I shine a light on AI and help leaders in life science and lab technology see the best path forward towards adoption. No hype, no confusion, just clear, practical guidance grounded in real scientific understanding.

Based in Ireland and serving clients worldwide, Lighthouse AI Consulting brings deep domain expertise to every engagement, whether you're across the road or across the ocean.
