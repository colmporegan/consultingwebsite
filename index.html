My story

What is Lighthouse AI Consulting?

The name Lighthouse takes its inspiration from the lighthouse that stands on Fastnet Rock off the southwest coast of Ireland. For over a century, it has guided ships safely through some of the most challenging waters in the North Atlantic.

That's the idea behind Lighthouse AI Consulting. I shine a light on AI and help business leaders see the best path forward towards adoption. No hype, no confusion, just clear, practical guidance that delivers real results.

Based in Ireland and serving clients worldwide, Lighthouse AI Consulting brings hands-on expertise to every engagement, whether you're across the road or across the ocean.

Frequently asked questions

Who do you typically work with?

Business leaders, marketing directors, operations managers, and teams across a range of industries who want to adopt AI but aren't sure where to start, or who have started but aren't seeing the results they expected.

What makes you different from other AI consultancies?

I focus on practical outcomes, not hype. Every engagement starts with understanding your business before recommending any technology. I work directly with you throughout, and I prioritise solutions based on real ROI and feasibility, not what's trendy.

Will AI actually work for my business?

That depends on where and how you apply it. That's exactly what the discovery call and readiness assessment are for. I'll help you identify where AI can genuinely make a difference and where it's not worth the investment.

How do you handle compliance and security concerns?

Every engagement includes a review of compliance and security requirements relevant to your sector. I build AI governance frameworks that keep humans in the loop at critical checkpoints and ensure your AI use meets ethical and regulatory standards.

What does a typical engagement look like?

It depends on where you are. Some clients start with a discovery call and readiness assessment. Others jump straight into strategy and roadmap development. We'll scope it together on an initial call.

Do you work with specific industries?

I work across industries. My background is in science and technology, but the AI strategy, implementation, and governance frameworks I build are applicable to any business looking to adopt AI responsibly and effectively.
